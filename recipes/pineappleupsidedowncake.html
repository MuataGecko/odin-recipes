Pineapple Upsidedown Cake

An example picture of Pineapple Upsidedown Cake

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 20 oz can pinapple sliced in juice, drained, juice reserved
  • 1 6 oz jar maraschino cherries without stems, drained
  • 1 15.25 oz box of yellow cake mix
    • ingredients called for on cake mix box

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°F
  2. In 13x9-inch pan, melt butter in oven
  3. Sprinkle brown sugar evenly over butter
  4. Arrange pineapple slices on brown sugar
  5. Place cherry in center of each pinapple slice and arrange remaining cherries around slices; press gently into brown sugar
  6. Add enough water to pineapple juice to measure one cup.
  7. Make cake batter as directed on box, substituting pineapple jice mixture for the water
  8. Pour batter over pineapple and cherries
  9. Bake 42 to 48 minutes
  10. Immediately run knife around side of pan to loosen cake and place heatproof serving plate upside down onto pan.
  11. Turn plate and pan over and let sit for 5 minutes
  12. Remove pan
